I am a Tutor

Passion Connect empowers rural students to connect with international volunteer tutors by matching them based on shared passions and interests. Students explore their curiosity; tutors share their expertise—together creating a world of inspired learning.

Our Approach Is Backed by Proven Research

Peer tutoring doesn’t just feel good—it works. Studies show that middle school students who participate in peer tutoring improve significantly across subjects, with effect sizes as high as 0.72—making a real difference in their academic performance.
